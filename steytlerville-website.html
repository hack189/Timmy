About Steytlerville

A small town with a strong sense of community. Below are a few highlights about location, governance and local life.

📍 Location

Steytlerville is located in the Eastern Cape, part of the Sarah Baartman District Municipality. The town is roughly 348 km south of Bhisho and serves as a hub for nearby farming communities.

👥 Community

A welcoming community with diverse neighbourhoods and active civic groups. Local sport, culture and markets form the backbone of social life.

🏛️ Local Government

Services and infrastructure are administered under the Sarah Baartman District. Municipal offices handle permits, local projects and community support programmes.
